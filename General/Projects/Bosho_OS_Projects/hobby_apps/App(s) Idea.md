## Basic Ideation
Consider all the small models that can run on a phone out there that do various things
	1) LLMs small models that can do inference locally. What can we do with this?
		1) Allow users to chat privately (is this proposition string enough??)
		2) What functionalities can we build around the SLMs on user devices?
			1) Autocorrect
			2) dictionary
			3) Chat with PDF
			4) Summarization
			5) Analyse messages or emails or something like that?
			6) Something like a local automation? 
	2) OCR and vision models:
		1) Scan documents and convert them into digital information in neat markdowns and PDFs. Neat extraction AI powered??
	3) Audio models?
		1) Transcriptions and recoding notes and so on?
		2) Convert conversation into written notes without sharing the data?
	4) Can we do this with local models on PC/Mac? what about as browser extension and so on? 
	5) What functionalities can we offer with APIs and non local models? What do people usually consume?
	6) How can we tie this to some of the most popular apps?
		1) Twitter?
		2) LinkedIn
		3) IG/FB/WhatsApp
		4) TikTok
		5) FB
	7) Can we do something for businesses and business owners? I think People pay a lot for classifieds? Like in Kenya: Something like Jiji or a price hunter or something like that?
