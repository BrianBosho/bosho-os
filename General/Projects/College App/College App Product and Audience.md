# College App Product and Audience

Source: [[../../00 Inbox/Workbook q2 wk1|Workbook q2 wk1]]

## Product work

- Create a Notion page for the eligibility report and the student user journey.
- Document features, services, and clear usage steps.
- Help students build shortlists of courses and share them cleanly.
- Produce polished PDFs and useful program links.

## Audience and growth ideas

- Build an audience capture channel starting with Twitter and then WhatsApp.
- Replace dependence on ads with useful content and audience aggregation.
- Create a content strategy around:
  - KUCCPS and university updates
  - student tips and tricks
  - weekly newsletter ideas
  - course spotlights
  - university spotlights

## Additional strategic ideas

- Use existing university students as a resource base.
- Explore certificates, payments, or incentives for contributors.
- Create a robust process for curating information and publishing updates.

## Operational notes from the source doc

- Hide model thinking output in student-facing chat.
- Add crash and usage monitoring, potentially with Sentry.
- Create a pipeline for features and services to implement next.
- Keep a business-development page with strategy and OKRs.
