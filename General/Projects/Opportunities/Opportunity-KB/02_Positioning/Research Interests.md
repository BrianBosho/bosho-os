# Research Interests

## Primary interests
- computer vision
- multimodal AI
- grounded perception
- trustworthy and reliable AI
- real-world robustness under distribution shift
- representation learning
- vision foundation models

## Secondary interests
- AI for science
- scientific imaging
- urban mobility and traffic-scene understanding
- efficient and low-resource AI
- federated and communication-constrained learning
- embodied and language-grounded systems

## Research questions of interest
- how can visual and multimodal systems become more grounded and reliable?
- how do models behave under real-world noise and shift?
- how can representation and geometry help diagnose model failure?
- how can robust AI systems be built for low-resource and high-stakes contexts?
