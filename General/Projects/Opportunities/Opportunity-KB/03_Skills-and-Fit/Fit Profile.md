# Fit Profile

## Great fit
- PhD roles in computer vision, multimodal learning, grounded perception, trustworthy AI
- research engineer roles in AI labs
- ML engineer roles requiring both modeling and implementation
- computer vision engineer roles
- AI for science / imaging roles
- applied AI roles with strong Python + backend + model evaluation components

## Easily qualifies for
- ML Engineer
- Applied AI Engineer
- AI Engineer
- Research Assistant / Research Engineer
- Computer Vision Engineer
- LLM systems engineer roles with backend/product implementation

## Uniquely qualified for
- opportunities needing both research depth and shipping ability
- roles combining computer vision, multimodal AI, and real-world deployment
- Africa- or low-resource-context AI work
- applied research roles where strong narrative coherence matters

## Stretch but worth trying
- highly competitive funded PhDs
- research scientist track-adjacent positions
- top research labs with strong topic fit
- Europe visa-sponsored research/ML roles
- competitive AI fellowships

## Not a good fit
- pure frontend engineering
- generic web development
- pure data analyst roles
- non-ML software roles with no modeling component
- heavily ops-only infrastructure roles

## Underqualified for
- senior staff/principal ML positions
- roles expecting a long top-tier publication record
- highly specialized roles with deep narrow-domain requirements not matched by current experience
