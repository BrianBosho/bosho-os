# Country Visa Notes

> Quick reference for agents scoring immigration feasibility. For full details see Immigration_and_Mobility.md.

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 10 | Remote or Kenya — no visa |
| 9 | Fully funded PhD — student visa standard |
| 8 | EU job with confirmed sponsorship record |
| 7 | EU Blue Card / Highly Skilled Migrant — very likely |
| 6 | Sponsorship possible — needs confirmation |
| 4 | Unlikely but not impossible |
| 0 | Citizens only / No sponsorship — hard stop |

## Country Quick Reference

### Germany 🇩🇪
- **PhD:** Residence permit tied to enrollment + scholarship. Standard for international funded PhD. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** EU Blue Card — requires salary ≥ ~45,000 EUR/yr (lower for STEM shortage occupations). Many German tech companies sponsor. Score: 7-8 if role explicitly sponsors.
- **Process:** Apply at German embassy in Kenya or Rwanda. Takes 4-12 weeks.
- **Key advantage:** Blue Card leads to permanent residence after 2-3 years.

### Netherlands 🇳🇱
- **PhD:** Dutch residence permit via university. Funded PhD = standard. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** Highly Skilled Migrant (HSM) permit — requires salary ≥ ~46,000 EUR/yr (lower for under-30). Netherlands is very international, many companies sponsor. Score: 8 if role explicitly sponsors.
- **Key advantage:** Very employer-friendly permit system; fast processing.

### France 🇫🇷
- **PhD:** Carte de séjour étudiant via INRIA/CNRS/university. Standard for funded PhD. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** EU Blue Card or Talent Passport — tech companies sponsor but less common than Germany/NL. Score: 6-7.
- **Note:** French language may be needed for some roles (not all).

### United Kingdom 🇬🇧
- **PhD:** Tier 4 Student Visa — standard with university offer. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** Skilled Worker Visa — employer must be on Home Office sponsor register. Many tech companies are. Score: 7 if confirmed sponsor.
- **Global Talent Visa:** Possible post-PhD with research track record. Score: N/A now, 8+ post-PhD.
- **Note:** Post-Brexit UK is not EU — separate from EU rules.

### Belgium 🇧🇪
- **PhD:** Student residence permit via institution. Standard. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** EU Blue Card or Single Permit. Score: 6-7.

### Switzerland 🇨🇭
- **PhD:** B-permit for non-EU via ETH/EPFL. Standard for fully funded PhD students. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** Very competitive, strict quotas for non-EU. Score: 5.

### Sweden 🇸🇪
- **PhD:** PhD students are employees in Sweden — work permit tied to PhD contract. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** Work permit possible for shortage occupations. Score: 6-7.

### Canada 🇨🇦
- **PhD:** Study permit + SIN tied to university enrollment. Standard. Score: 9
- **Jobs:** Express Entry for permanent residence after work experience. Score: 7 if employer sponsors.
- **PGWP:** Post-graduation work permit available after study.

### Kenya 🇰🇪
- **Score: 10** — Full right to work as citizen. No visa needed.

### Remote (any country) 🌐
- **Score: 10** — No immigration barrier if company permits hiring from Rwanda/Kenya.
- **Note:** Some companies restrict remote hiring by country for tax/legal reasons — verify.

### United States 🇺🇸
- **PhD:** F-1 student visa. Possible but US deprioritized.
- **Jobs:** H-1B lottery — very unreliable. Score: 3. Do not prioritize.

### Rwanda 🇷🇼
- **Score: 10** — Currently resident. EAC mobility applies.
- Brian can work for Rwanda-based organizations without additional visa.
