# Interests

## Primary Research Interests

- **Visual groundedness in VLMs** — when and why multimodal models rely on language priors vs genuine visual evidence
- **Information-theoretic diagnostics** — measuring what information flows through model layers (Visual Information Gain, IB-style bottlenecks)
- **Spectral manifold analysis** — using Laplacian eigenmodes and diffusion geometry to detect representation pathologies in transformers
- **Causal representation learning** — framing "right for the right reason" as causal sufficiency, not just accuracy
- **Robust perception** — models that hold up under noise, distribution shift, weak labels, and adversarial degradation
- **Federated and efficient learning** — privacy-aware, communication-efficient training (FedProp background)

## Secondary Research Interests

- AI for scientific discovery (biomedical imaging, Cryo-ET, structural biology)
- Urban mobility and traffic scene understanding in low-resource environments
- Multimodal foundation models and their failure modes
- Embodied agents and physical grounding
- Geometric deep learning (graph neural networks, scattering transforms)
- Test-time adaptation and continual learning for deployed models

## Applied and Systems Interests

- Building production AI systems that combine modeling and deployment
- LLM-powered applications with retrieval, tool-calling, grounded response generation
- AI in low-resource and African contexts
- Full-stack AI application development

## Long-Term Mission

To advance the foundations of robust multimodal perception AND create pathways for African researchers to participate in frontier AI research. Sees these as mutually reinforcing, not competing.

## Things Brian Finds Genuinely Exciting

- Mathematical rigor applied to practical AI systems
- Bridging theory and engineering — not just one or the other
- Research that holds up to scrutiny in deployment, not just on benchmarks
- AI that works in the real world, especially where resources are constrained
- Mentoring and building research community in Africa
