# Bio — Short (50 words)

Brian Kipkirui is a Research Associate at Carnegie Mellon University Africa and M.S. AI Valedictorian (GPA 3.97). His research focuses on visual groundedness in multimodal foundation models, using information-theoretic and spectral geometric methods to diagnose when vision-language models rely on language priors rather than genuine visual evidence.
