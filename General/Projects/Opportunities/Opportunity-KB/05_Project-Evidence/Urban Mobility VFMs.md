# Urban Mobility VFMs

## One-line summary
Research on vision foundation models and real-world traffic-scene understanding in low-resource urban African environments.

## Problem
Real traffic scenes in low-resource settings are complex, noisy, and poorly matched by standard benchmark assumptions.

## What Brian did
- built and curated urban mobility datasets for Kigali
- designed benchmarking workflows
- evaluated robustness, grounding, and failure modes of vision models under non-standard traffic conditions
