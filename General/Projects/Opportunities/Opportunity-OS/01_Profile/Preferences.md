# Preferences

## Role Type Preferences (ranked)

1. **PhD position** — top priority across all searches
2. **Research fellowship** — especially with stipend and mobility
3. **Research Engineer / Applied Scientist** — at strong AI lab or startup
4. **ML Engineer** — with real modeling depth, not just serving
5. **Remote AI/ML job** — flexible, income-generating, parallel to PhD search
6. **Kenya-based job** — meaningful work, local ecosystem building
7. **Part-time / contract** — acceptable as bridge income

## Environment Preferences

- **High-quality research** — rigorous, publications matter, not just demos
- **Strong supervision** — active advisor/mentor, not hands-off
- **Technical depth** — not shallow product/marketing AI
- **Open science culture** — publishes, shares, collaborates
- **Socially relevant AI** — not optimizing ad CTR
- **Mathematical grounding** — values theory alongside experiments
- **International / diverse team** — comfortable in global settings

- **PhD stipend minimum:** ~1,500 EUR/month or equivalent (fully funded essential)
- **Remote job minimum:** $3,000 USD/month gross
- **Kenya job minimum:** KES 300,000/month gross
- **Fellowship:** Case-by-case, but cost of living must be covered
