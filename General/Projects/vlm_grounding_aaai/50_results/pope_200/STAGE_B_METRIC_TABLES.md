# Stage B Ablations — Full Metric Tables

AUC scores for every metric across all three space conditions and four evaluation targets.

**Conditions:**
- `raw` = B2_S0_raw (no whitening)
- `all` = B2_S1_all (whitened, full union covariance pool)
- `bal` = B2_S1_bal (whitened, balanced 200-per-family pool)

**Targets:**
- `HY` = `hallucinated_yes` (primary)
- `IC` = `is_correct`
- `WY` = `within_yes`
- `WN` = `within_no`

Bold = highest AUC among the three conditions for that metric × target cell.
