# Archival Rules

## Durable vs Temporary

Permanent notes should capture stable patterns, important decisions, systems, or refined insights.

Temporary thoughts belong in inbox or daily notes.

## Distillation

Move information upward:

Daily Notes → Reflections → Systems/Codex

## Historical Integrity

Do not erase old perspectives casually.
Prefer updating and annotating.

## Preferred Unit

Atomic notes connected through links.
