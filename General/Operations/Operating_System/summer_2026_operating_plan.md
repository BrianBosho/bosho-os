# Summer 2026 Operating Plan

## Mission

This summer is for four things:

1. **Close old research loops** — especially the TMLR FedProp paper.
2. **Build the PhD trajectory** — especially VLM grounding, AI safety, multimodal self-evaluation, and strong people/lab positioning.
3. **Restore the worker** — sleep, cardio, strength, diet, rest days, psychiatric monitoring, meditation, social connection.
4. **Build an AI-augmented operating system** — Codex/OpenClaw/ChatGPT workflows that reduce friction and turn ideas into execution.

## Core mantra

> Hard days hard. Rest days real. Work bounded. Sleep protected. Old loops closed. Future direction built.

## Weekly rhythm

The summer defaults to a **4 + 3 rhythm**:

- **4 intense work days**
- **3 restoration / maintenance days**
- Work days can contain up to three 3-hour work blocks.
- Rest days are not empty days; they maintain the worker.

## Workday definition

A workday is successful when it has:

- 2–3 bounded work blocks.
- A clear shutdown.
- Exercise/cardio.
- Food.
- Meditation or wind-down.
- A next slot assigned for every unfinished task.

## Restoration day definition

A restoration day is successful when it includes some of:

- Sleep and wake consistency.
- Lifting/cardio/walking.
- Cleaning/laundry/groceries.
- Meal prep.
- Reading.
- Meditation/reflection.
- Family/friends/social contact.
- Weekly or midweek processing.

## Non-negotiable principle

Work must be bounded. If a task has no defined scope, it should not start. If a task is unfinished, the next slot must be assigned before stopping.

## Summer success criteria

By the end of summer:

- TMLR FedProp is submitted, shared, or clearly resolved.
- VLM grounding has a concrete paper direction, repo/notebook, experiment plan, and team rhythm.
- PhD prep has a reading list, people map, fellowships list, and logistics tracker.
- Acubed has a clearer product/R&D rhythm with transcriber and DSS tracks.
- Swyng has either active operations or a clean delegated plan.
- Sleep, cardio, diet, meditation, and rest days are functioning as a base.
- The AI-agent system has become normal: every repo/project has prompts, templates, and repeatable workflows.
