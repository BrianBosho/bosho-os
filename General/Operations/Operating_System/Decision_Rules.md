# Decision Rules

## Rules

- If a plan feels self-deceptive after two honest minutes, shrink it.
- If something can be fixed by structure, do not over-analyze it.
- Prefer one real artifact over a vague work session.
- Prefer reliability over heroic output.
- Do not redesign the entire life while basic rhythm is unstable.
- Do not let a serious research project remain solo for too long.

## One-Liners Worth Keeping

- "Sleep early -> wake early -> think before the world interferes."
- "I earn the right to analyze by first executing."
- "Structure is internal safety, not productivity theater."
