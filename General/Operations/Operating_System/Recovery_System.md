# Recovery System

## Definition

Recovery is not merely stopping activity. It is restoration of capacity.

## Required Layers

- Physical: sleep, food, movement, sunlight, lower tension.
- Cognitive: time where nothing is being optimized or solved.
- Emotional: less urgency and less self-monitoring.
- Existential: not asking each day to justify the whole life.

## Working Rules

- Recovery should be treated as infrastructure for good work.
- Rest that is secretly planning or evaluating is not recovery.
- The pre-PhD phase should prioritize repair over output maximization.

## Signs Of Real Recovery

- Lower baseline urgency.
- Better sleep regularity.
- More willingness to start work calmly.
- More stable attention.
- Less need to constantly re-theorize life.
