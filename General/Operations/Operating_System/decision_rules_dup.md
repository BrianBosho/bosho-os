# Decision Rules

## 1. Bound work before starting

Before any serious task:

- What is the exact output?
- What is forbidden during this block?
- What counts as done?
- What is the next slot if unfinished?

If these are unclear, the task is not ready.

## 2. Do not confuse meaning with obligation

A project can be meaningful and still not be active this week.

## 3. Closure before expansion

Until TMLR is cleanly shared/submitted/resolved, new research ideas cannot consume prime cognition.

## 4. Prime cognition goes to strategic work

Morning and early afternoon blocks go to:

1. TMLR while unresolved.
2. VLM/AAAI after TMLR is stable.
3. PhD trajectory work.

Admin, coordination, messages, and tool tinkering go later.

## 5. Rest days are protected

A rest day is not a failed workday. It is a worker-maintenance day.

## 6. Agents reduce friction, not responsibility

AI agents can inspect, draft, refactor, summarize, test, and organize. Brian makes the intellectual and ethical decisions.

## 7. Every open loop gets a status

- Completed
- Rescheduled
- Removed
- Waiting on someone
- Not this week
