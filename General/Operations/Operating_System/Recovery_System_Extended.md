# Recovery System

## Philosophy

Recovery is not the absence of work but the restoration of clarity, nervous system stability, vitality, and psychological spaciousness.

## Weekly Recovery Structure

- One work-free restoration day per week
- Long sleep and slow mornings
- Hiking, walking, swimming, or gentle movement
- Reading without pressure
- Organization and environment reset
- Reflection and stillness
