# Weekly Rhythm: 4 Work Days / 3 Restoration Days

## Default week

| Day | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Intense work | Deep research / closure |
| Tuesday | Intense work | Deep research / writing / experiments |
| Wednesday | Restoration + maintenance | Gym, meal prep, errands, reading, processing |
| Thursday | Intense work | VLM / PhD / Acubed |
| Friday | Intense work | Research closure + weekly review |
| Saturday | Active restoration | Long cardio/gym, social, reading, cleaning |
| Sunday | Full restoration + reset | Spiritual reset, family, weekly planning |

## Workday block structure

A full workday has up to three work blocks:

1. **Block 1:** hardest intellectual task.
2. **Block 2:** continuation or second-hardest task.
3. **Block 3:** lower-friction work: writing, admin, coordination, documentation, analysis.

A normal successful day is **2 deep blocks + 1 support block**.  
A heroic day is **3 deep blocks**.  
A minimum viable workday is **1 deep block + clean shutdown**.

## Guardrail

Rest days may absorb admin, errands, cleaning, meal prep, exercise, reading, family, and review.  
They may not absorb deep research work unless there is an actual external deadline within 72 hours.
