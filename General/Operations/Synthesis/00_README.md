# Planner Knowledge Base

This directory is the second-pass synthesis of the planner vault. It is meant to preserve the knowledge in a stable form, not merely archive raw conversations.

Contents:

- `01_IDENTITY_AND_WORLDVIEW.md`: stable identity, values, and long-horizon orientation.
- `02_CURRENT_REALITY_AT_CAPTURE.md`: current-state snapshot inferred from the notes.
- `03_RECURRING_PATTERNS_AND_FAILURE_MODES.md`: repeating loops, constraints, and risks.
- `04_OPERATING_SYSTEM_AND_RECOVERY.md`: practical rules for structure, recovery, and execution.
- `05_RESEARCH_AND_PHD_STRATEGY.md`: PhD, research, and technical-direction synthesis.
- `06_SPIRITUAL_FRAMEWORK.md`: Buddhist, Vipassana, and philosophical operating frame.
- `07_SOURCE_MAP_AND_DUPLICATE_REVIEW.md`: where the synthesis came from and what still needs consolidation.

Rules:

- Treat these files as the durable layer.
- Treat `_knowledge_extraction/` as the audit and machine-readable layer.
- Do not delete original notes until this knowledge base has been reviewed and any missing material has been folded in.
