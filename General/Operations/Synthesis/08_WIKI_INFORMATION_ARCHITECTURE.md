# Wiki Information Architecture

This is the recommended structure for turning the vault into a comprehensive personal wiki and durable knowledge base.

## What Information Is In The Vault

The vault is not random. Most of it falls into six stable domains:

1. Identity, worldview, and meaning
2. Current state, constraints, and life transition
3. Recurring patterns, failure modes, and self-regulation
4. Operating systems for work, recovery, structure, and execution
5. Research, PhD, AI safety, and technical development
6. Buddhist, Vipassana, and philosophical interpretation

There are also two smaller classes:

- tool/workflow notes that were misgrouped under unrelated titles
- duplicate or near-duplicate saved variants

## Best Top-Level Wiki Structure

Use a small number of stable root sections:

```text
Wiki/
├── 00_Home.md
├── 01_Identity/
├── 02_Current_Reality/
├── 03_Patterns/
├── 04_Operating_System/
├── 05_Research_And_Career/
├── 06_Spiritual_Framework/
├── 07_Health_And_Energy/
├── 08_Decisions_And_Timelines/
├── 09_Sources_And_Archive/
└── 10_Indexes/
```

## What Each Section Should Hold

### `00_Home.md`

The wiki front door.

Include:

- one-paragraph identity summary
- current top priorities
- links to the main overview pages
- recent major decisions
- major unresolved questions

### `01_Identity/`

Stable traits and deep orientation.

Pages:

- `Identity_Overview.md`
- `Values_And_Principles.md`
- `Long_Horizon_Vision.md`
- `Strengths_And_Advantages.md`
- `Tensions_And_Contradictions.md`

Source material:

- identity and worldview
- desired legacy
- ambition vs peace
- stillness in motion
- worth beyond productivity

### `02_Current_Reality/`

Snapshot pages that can change over time.

Pages:

- `Current_Life_Context.md`
- `Current_Constraints.md`
- `Current_Opportunities.md`
- `PhD_Launch_Context.md`
- `Current_Open_Loops.md`

This section should be updated regularly. It is not the place for timeless truths.

### `03_Patterns/`

Psychological, behavioral, and situational patterns.

Pages:

- `Core_Pressure_Loop.md`
- `Self_Trust_Erosion.md`
- `Planning_As_Avoidance.md`
- `Overload_And_Fragmentation.md`
- `Isolation_Risk.md`
- `Failure_Modes.md`
- `What_Actually_Helps.md`

This is one of the most important sections because it converts repeated conversations into reusable pattern knowledge.

### `04_Operating_System/`

Rules, routines, and execution systems.

Pages:

- `Daily_System.md`
- `Weekly_System.md`
- `Recovery_System.md`
- `Deep_Work_System.md`

This should contain practical operating rules, not reflection-heavy prose.

### `05_Research_And_Career/`

The PhD and research track.

Pages:

- `Technical_Skill_Development.md`

- `Collaboration_System.md`

This section should connect long-term ambition to concrete research execution.

### `06_Spiritual_Framework/`

Spiritual and philosophical material integrated into life design.

Pages:

- `Buddhist_Operating_Principles.md`
- `Vipassana_For_Anxiety_And_Pressure.md`

This section should preserve the concepts without letting them float free of practical life.

### `07_Health_And_Energy/`

Sleep, energy, body, medication, stress physiology.

Pages:

- `Sleep_System.md`
- `Energy_Management.md`
- `Stress_And_Tension.md`
- `Recovery_Basics.md`
- `Medication_Notes.md`

This is separate from the operating system because it tracks the organism itself.

### `08_Decisions_And_Timelines/`

Decision records and temporal context.

Pages:

- `Major_Decisions.md`
- `PhD_Decision_Log.md`
- `Timeline_Of_Concerns.md`
- `Turning_Points.md`

Use this to separate "what happened and when" from timeless synthesis.

### `09_Sources_And_Archive/`

Provenance layer.

Pages:

- `Source_Map.md`
- `Duplicate_Review.md`
- `Conversation_To_Page_Map.md`

This section should point back to the raw notes and extracted files.

### `10_Indexes/`

Cross-cutting access pages.

Pages:

- `Topic_Index.md`
- `People_Index.md`
- `Concept_Index.md`
- `System_Index.md`
- `Open_Questions_Index.md`

## Recommended Page Types

Use only a few page types consistently:

- Overview pages: high-level synthesis
- Pattern pages: recurring loops and signals
- System pages: rules and operating procedures
- Decision logs: what was chosen and why
- Source maps: where the knowledge came from

Avoid turning the wiki into:

- a daily planner
- a task tracker
- a giant journal
- a dump of raw conversations

## Best Next Build Sequence

Build the wiki in this order:

1. Create `00_Home.md`
2. Split the current synthesis pages into the section structure above
3. Build 10-15 high-value permanent pages
4. Add decision logs and timeline pages
5. Add cross-indexes
6. Only then archive or retire raw source conversations

## Final Recommendation

Treat this as a personal encyclopedia, not a planner.

The right design is:

- stable synthesis pages at the center
- source notes kept for provenance
- patterns and systems extracted into reusable reference pages
- current reality and decisions tracked separately from timeless identity

That will give you a wiki that is actually navigable, cumulative, and safe to maintain over time.
