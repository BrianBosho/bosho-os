# Shutdown Protocol

## Purpose

Work ends by closure, not collapse.

## Daily Shutdown Checklist

- [ ] What did I complete today?
- [ ] What remains open?
- [ ] What is the next action?
- [ ] When is the next slot?
- [ ] What can be removed or postponed?
- [ ] Is tomorrow clear enough?
- [ ] Are tabs/files closed or organized?
- [ ] Is the workspace reset?

## Script

> The day is complete enough. Open loops have homes. The next actions are assigned. I do not need to keep carrying this mentally tonight.

## Output

At the end of shutdown, there should be no vague work floating in the head.
