# Rest Day Protocol

## Purpose

Rest days maintain the worker. They are not failed workdays.

## Types

### Wednesday: Restoration + Maintenance

- Gym/cardio.
- Cleaning.
- Laundry.
- Groceries.
- Meal prep.
- Errands.
- Reading.
- Meditation.

### Saturday: Active Restoration

- Long cardio or lifting.
- Meal/social/outing.
- Reading outside.
- Light life admin.
- Family/friend contact.

### Sunday: Full Reset

- No serious work.
- Reflection.
- Spiritual/philosophical reading.
- Weekly planning.
- Family connection.
- Early sleep.

## Rest Day Closure Entry

Before rest day begins, process open loops:

- Completed
- Rescheduled
- Removed
- Waiting on someone
- Not this week

## Forbidden Unless True Deadline Within 72 Hours

- Deep research work.
- New project commitments.
- Heroic catch-up.
- Starting a new work thread.

## Good Rest Day Activities

- Walk.
- Lift.
- Zone 2 cardio.
- Meal prep.
- Read a book in a cafe.
- Meditate.
- Call family.
- Clean room.
- Journal.
- Do nothing deliberately.
