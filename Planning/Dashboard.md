## Weekly rule

The week is controlled by containers:

- 8:00-11:00: FedProp.
- 13:00-16:00: VLM, Colab, or FedProp overflow.
- 16:30-18:30: exercise, outside, or recovery.
- 19:30-21:00: one logistics theme.

Do not use this dashboard to add more active work. Use it to preserve the current week.
