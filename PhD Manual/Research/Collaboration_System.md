# Collaboration System

## Core Rule

No serious PhD project should remain a solo project for more than 3-4 weeks.

## Three Circles

- Anchors: people who protect direction and stop drift.
- Active collaborators: people building papers or experiments with you.
- Weak ties: warm research contacts who may open future doors.

## Working Method

- Ask people to react to something concrete.
- Send memos, results, figures, or evaluation ideas.
- Track status and next action.
- Follow up clearly, not anxiously.

## Minimal Collaboration Board

- Name
- Role
- Fit
- Status
- Next action
- Last contact
