# Technical Skill Development

## What Matters

Professors and collaborators care about evidence of real implementation ability, reproducibility, and engineering maturity.

## Priority Areas

- Python and ML research engineering.
- Clean experiment structure.
- Config and environment hygiene.
- Logging and reproducibility.
- Paper reimplementation.
- Small benchmark or evaluation artifacts.

## What Impresses People

- Initiative.
- Clean repos.
- Clear documentation.
- Modular implementation.
- Insightful questions tied to experiments.

## Build Rule

Prefer repeated concrete implementations over vague self-assessment of technical level.
