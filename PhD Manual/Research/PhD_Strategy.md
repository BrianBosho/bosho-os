# PhD Strategy

## Strategic Frame

Treat the PhD as a five-year authority engine, not a passive credential.

## Entry Conditions For A Good Path

- It strengthens technical rigor.
- It increases credibility.
- It expands collaborators and network.
- It allows concrete artifact production.
- It preserves future optionality.

## Main Risks

- Entering already depleted.
- Waiting passively for structure.
- Engineering weakness becoming a ceiling.
- Isolation around projects.
- Losing coherence across research directions.

## Practical Aim

Enter with a stable base, an emerging research line, and a bias toward building concrete artifacts that other people can react to.
