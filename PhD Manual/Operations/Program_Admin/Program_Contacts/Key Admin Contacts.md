## CMU ECE — Academic Affairs

### Greta Ruperto
**Role:** PhD Senior Academic Program Advisor  
**Department:** ECE Academic Affairs Office, Carnegie Mellon University  
**When to contact:**
- Coursework questions and approvals
- Petitions (milestone extensions, breadth substitutions, course transfers)
- Qualifying exam logistics and declarations
- Teaching Internship (TI) facilitation
- Taking >2 technical courses per semester (requires her + advisor written authorization)
- Withdrawal from a course after add/drop deadline
- Any policy question about ECE PhD requirements

*She is the central administrative contact for ECE PhD students. Schedule a meeting before submitting any petition.*
