# FCT Scholarship

Source: Dual-Degree PhD Student Guide 2025–26, Admission Letter

## Overview

The FCT (Fundação para a Ciência e a Tecnologia) scholarship funds the Portugal period of the dual-degree program. FCT is Portugal's national science foundation.

**Monthly stipend:** €1,359.64/month  
**CMU stipend (Pittsburgh period):** $43,800/year (separate from FCT; provided by CMU program)

---

## Allowances

| Allowance | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Travel allowance | €600 | Upon leaving Portugal to CMU |
| Moving allowance | €1,000 | Upon moving to Portugal |
| Research visit support | €750/month | For visits to other institutions |
| Scientific meetings | €750 | Per conference/meeting |

## FCT Renewal Process

**The FCT contract is not renewed automatically.** When the scholarship is active during any period totaling 12 months at a Portuguese institution, a RENEWAL request is required.

**Steps:**

1. Email CMU Portugal coordination (info@cmuportugal.org) **at least 90 days before** the scholarship renewal date with:
   - Expected month for beginning of renewal
   - Scholarship reference number (SFRH/BD/.../...) from the FCT contract
   - Short paragraph on coursework and research developed

2. Attach the following documents:
   - Statement from each advisor confirming agreement with renewal + short progress note
   - Up-to-date declaration of professional situation (employment status, teaching hours) — FCT has a template
   - Statement from host institution on student's work and progress
   - Document from Portuguese HEI confirming current Ph.D. registration
   - Declaration of contributions to Portuguese social security (if applicable) — via www.seg-social.pt

3. Student submits the completed request on **MyFCT** (advisors receive an email from FCT to fill in their statement on MyFCT)

4. Notify CMU Portugal (info@cmuportugal.org) once FCT approves, specifying the approval date

## Internship Rules

Internships during the FCT-funded period must be checked against:
- FCT scholarship rules (suspension may be required)
- OIE/CPT rules if returning to the US
- Advisor approval
- CMU|Portugal program compatibility

Check the CMU Portugal coordination office before confirming any internship arrangement.

## Acknowledgment Requirement

All publications, presentations, posters, and communications produced under FCT funding **must acknowledge FCT and CMU Portugal**.

**Required English text:**
> "This work is co-financed by Fundação para a Ciência e a Tecnologia (Portuguese Foundation for Science and Technology) through the Carnegie Mellon Portugal Program under the fellowship (insert FCT grant reference SFRH/BD/xxxxxx/20xx)."

**Required Portuguese text:**
> "Este trabalho é cofinanciado pela Fundação para a Ciência e a Tecnologia, através do Programa Carnegie Mellon Portugal no âmbito da bolsa (inserir referência da bolsa FCT SFRH/BD/xxxxxx/20xx)."

FCT and CMU Portugal logos must also appear on posters, presentations, and brochures. Download logos from cmuportugal.org and fct.pt.
