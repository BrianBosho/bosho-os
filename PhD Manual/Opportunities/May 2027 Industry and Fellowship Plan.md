# May 2027 Industry and Fellowship Plan

## Purpose

Prepare for an industry research opportunity, AI safety fellowship, or related research program around the May 2027 break.

The goal is to avoid scrambling by building eligibility, artifacts, relationships, and advisor compatibility early.

## Target Window

May 2027 onward.

## Opportunity Types

- Industry research internships
- AI safety fellowships
- Interpretability research programs
- VLM evaluation / safety research placements
- Visiting research collaborations
- Part-time remote mentorship programs

## Fit Criteria

Strong opportunities should satisfy most of:

- connects to VLM grounding, self-evaluation, interpretability, evaluations, or AI safety
- produces research output or strong feedback
- is compatible with CMU/FEUP/FCT/OIE constraints
- strengthens the PhD rather than replacing it with unrelated work
- creates useful relationships with researchers or labs

## Qualification Gaps

| Gap | Why it matters | Next action |
|---|---|---|
| Concrete research artifact | Fellowships and internships need evidence of execution | Finish active project brief and first experiment/result |
| Safety framing | AI safety programs need clear relevance | Write a one-page safety framing of VLM grounding |
| Publication or preprint | Strong signal for research roles | Identify a workshop/submission path |
| Writing sample | Needed for applications and outreach | Convert a synthesis memo or project brief into a polished sample |
| CV / website | Basic application infrastructure | Update materials in [[05_Opportunities/Application Materials Bank]] |
| Recommenders | Applications need credible external signal | Build execution record with supervisors/collaborators |
| AI safety background | Some programs expect field fluency | Consider ARENA, SPAR, MATS prep, or equivalent reading/program path |

## Required Artifacts

- Updated CV
- One-page research pitch
- Two-page active project brief
- Safety framing memo
- Writing sample
- Project page or GitHub page once results exist
- Recommender list
- Compatibility checklist for advisor, FEUP, FCT, CMU, OIE, visa, and IP constraints

## Backward Timeline

### June-August 2026

- Finish active project brief.
- Produce first concrete VLM grounding/self-evaluation artifact.
- Start reading and paper notes for safety/interp relevance.

### September-December 2026

- Build a stronger experiment table.

- Identify 2027 fellowship and internship cycles.

- Update CV and research statement.
- Draft application materials.
- Ask for recommender guidance.

- Prepare interviews or project discussions.

### May 2027

- Begin opportunity if accepted and compatible, or use break for a structured research sprint.
