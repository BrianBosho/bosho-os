## Featured Project

### FedProp: Federated Graph Neural Networks with Feature Propagation
*Under review at a major AI conference*

Cross-client edges in federated GNNs cause information gaps that degrade accuracy. FedProp propagates features iteratively to reconstruct l-hop neighborhood information — without any inter-client communication or additional parameters.

**Results:** 81.0% accuracy on Cora (non-IID), a 19.1% relative improvement over the federated baseline. Includes spectral graph theory convergence guarantee.

**My role:** Algorithm conception, theoretical analysis, implementation, and experimental evaluation.
