I don't just design algorithms — I build end-to-end pipelines that work. - **Data:** Curation, augmentation, cleaning (diffusion models for low-resource data)
- **Model:** Architecture design, training, evaluation (3D U-Net, GNNs, VFMs)

- **Deployment:** SLURM, AWS, edge inference considerations

### I Know Where AI Breaks
After working on Cryo-ET pipelines, urban traffic in Kigali, and VQA probing of VLMs, I have a visceral understanding of where current models fail. This is exactly the intuition needed to build products in biomedical AI, autonomous systems, or AI infrastructure — domains where failure is costly.
