## SOP Structure (from notes)

### Key Tips
1. Highlight **area of interest** (& possibly profs you want to work with)
2. Briefly describe **academics** (grades, honors, projects)
3. **Prior and ongoing research** — for each: motivation, role, contribution, outcome
4. Admissions committees read between the lines for **self-motivation, competence, potential**
5. **Demonstrate by example** — don't say you're persistent, show it
6. If something affected grades (poverty, illness, work) — state it affirmatively
7. Maintain **continuity and focus** across the narrative
8. Ideal length: **500–1000 words** (1–2 single-spaced pages in 12pt font)
