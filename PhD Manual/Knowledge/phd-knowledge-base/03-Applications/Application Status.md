## What Brian's target set reveals about his preferences

### He prefers labs that are:
- strong in **computer vision or multimodal learning**,
- serious about **grounding, geometry, or perception**,
- capable of supporting **causal or mechanistic research questions**,
- open to **scientific, biomedical, or safety-critical applications**.

### He is less excited by:
- purely generic LLM work,
- loosely specified AI programs without clear supervisor fit,
- labs that are adjacent to AI but weak on perception, grounding, or rigorous technical depth.

Brian's strongest applications are the ones where he is framed as:
