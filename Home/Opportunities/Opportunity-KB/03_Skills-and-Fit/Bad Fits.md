# Bad Fits

## Avoid prioritizing
- frontend-heavy product engineering
- generic CRUD backend roles with no ML component
- data analyst roles centered on dashboards / BI only
- senior MLOps roles where infra ownership is the main evaluation criterion
- roles requiring deep domain specialization without overlap to existing work

## Why
These do not leverage the strongest parts of Brian's profile: research depth, perception systems, multimodal AI, and ML engineering with technical substance.
