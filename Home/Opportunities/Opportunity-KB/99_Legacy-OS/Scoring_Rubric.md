# Scoring Rubric

> Agents: use this rubric to score every opportunity before any application work begins.
> No tailored documents are created until scoring is complete.

---

## The 7 Dimensions

Score each from 1–10.

### A. Interest Fit (weight: 0.25)
How aligned is this with Brian's genuine research and career interests?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Core research theme — visual grounding, multimodal AI, robust perception, geometric DL, causal ML |
| 7-8 | Strong adjacent area — applied CV, AI for science, federated learning, foundation models |
| 5-6 | Tangentially related — general ML, some vision component, interesting but not ideal |
| 3-4 | Weak alignment — data science, generic NLP, applied AI without research depth |
| 1-2 | No alignment — pure software, non-ML, unrelated domain |

### B. Qualification Fit (weight: 0.20)
How well do Brian's current skills and credentials match requirements?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Clear match — master's + research + tech stack explicitly valued |
| 7-8 | Strong match — most requirements met, one minor gap |
| 5-6 | Partial match — qualified for the role but some meaningful gaps |
| 3-4 | Underqualified — requires PhD, more publications, or years of experience Brian doesn't have |
| 1-2 | Clearly unqualified — wrong field, wrong level |

### C. Unique Fit (weight: 0.15)
Does Brian's unusual profile give him a real edge?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | His exact combination is rare and directly valued — valedictorian + spectral theory + VLMs + Africa context all matter |
| 7-8 | His background is meaningfully differentiated |
| 5-6 | Good candidate but not uniquely positioned |
| 3-4 | Generic candidate — his differentiators don't matter here |
| 1-2 | Disadvantaged relative to typical pool |

### D. Immigration Feasibility (weight: 0.15)
Can Brian realistically work/study there?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 10 | Remote or Kenya-based — no visa needed |
| 9 | Fully funded PhD — student visa standard and well-established |
| 7-8 | EU/UK job with confirmed or very likely sponsorship |
| 5-6 | EU/UK job with possible sponsorship — needs verification |
| 3-4 | Sponsorship unlikely or unclear — high risk |
| 1-2 | No sponsorship — infeasible |
| 0 | Explicitly "no visa" or "citizens only" — disqualify immediately |

### E. Strategic Value (weight: 0.15)
Would success here meaningfully advance Brian's long-term path?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Top PhD or fellowship — directly enables research career + long-term goals |
| 7-8 | Strong research engineer or applied scientist role — excellent stepping stone |
| 5-6 | Decent ML job — income + experience but not transformative |
| 3-4 | Fine job but off-path — does not build toward research career |
| 1-2 | Actively detracts — takes time, builds no relevant skills |

### F. Prestige / Upside (weight: 0.10)
How valuable is success here if it goes well?

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 9-10 | Top-tier institution or lab — Mila, ETH, ELLIS Tübingen, DeepMind, etc. |
| 7-8 | Strong institution or reputable company |
| 5-6 | Solid but not exceptional |
| 3-4 | Modest prestige |
| 1-2 | Low prestige, limited upside |

---

## Computed Overall Score

```
Overall = (0.25 × Interest) + (0.20 × Qualification) + (0.15 × UniquenessFit)
        + (0.15 × Immigration) + (0.15 × StrategicValue) + (0.10 × Prestige)
```

## Separate Flags (not in formula)

**Effort estimate:** Low / Medium / High / Very High
- Low: cover letter + CV tweak, <2 hours
- Medium: tailored letter + custom CV, 4-6 hours
- High: full application with SOP + references + transcripts, 10+ hours
- Very High: multi-stage with coding challenge, presentations, 20+ hours

**Urgency:** Deadline in days from today

---

## Decision Buckets

| Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| 8.5 – 10 | **Must apply** — prioritize immediately |
| 7.0 – 8.4 | **Strong apply** — proceed with tailored materials |
| 5.5 – 6.9 | **Conditional** — apply only if low effort or strategic reason |
| < 5.5 | **Skip** — unless near-zero effort (quick apply) |
| Immigration score = 0 | **Disqualify immediately** — do not proceed |
