# Visa Strategy

## Why this matters
Many attractive opportunities are only realistic if they support funded study, visa sponsorship, or remote arrangements.

## Agent guidance
When appraising an opportunity, check:
- is visa sponsorship explicit, likely, unclear, or absent?
- is this a student/funded route or employment route?
- is the country strategically desirable?
- does immigration feasibility materially reduce the value of the opportunity?

## Decision rule
A technically strong opportunity with poor immigration feasibility may still be kept, but should be rated lower unless strategic upside is extremely high.
