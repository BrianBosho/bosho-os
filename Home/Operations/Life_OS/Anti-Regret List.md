# Anti-Regret List

## Purpose

Prevent the PhD from becoming a narrow tunnel.

## I do not want to regret

- Spending years in Pittsburgh without really living there.
- Spending years in Portugal without exploring Portugal and Europe.
- Becoming technically strong but physically unhealthy.
- Producing papers but losing curiosity.
- Having access to brilliant people but not building relationships.
- Waiting for permission to pursue meaningful collaborations.
- Letting fear make me reactive.
- Treating life as something that begins after graduation.

## Anti-regret commitments

- Schedule life, not only work.
- Travel when it is reasonable, not only when everything is finished.
- Maintain at least one hobby without guilt.
- Build friendships outside direct research utility.
- Take care of health before collapse.
- Say yes to some experiences because they will become memories.

## Review question

> What would I regret if I lived the next six months exactly like the last month?
