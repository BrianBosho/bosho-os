# Anti-Burnout Protocol

## Warning signs

- I feel constantly behind.
- I stop reading because everything feels urgent.
- I stop talking to people.
- I avoid supervisors or collaborators.
- I cannot define my main project.
- I feel trapped by obligations.
- I stop exercising.
- I stop enjoying the city I live in.
- I feel guilty whenever I rest.
- I wake up already anxious.

## Trigger

If three or more signs appear, activate this protocol.

## Recovery protocol

1. Reduce scope for one week.
2. Write down all open loops.
3. Identify the true bottleneck.
4. Talk to one trusted person.
5. Contact advisor or collaborator if research direction is blocked.
6. Resume exercise and sleep before increasing workload.
7. Convert vague pressure into a concrete next action.
8. Schedule one non-work restorative experience.

## Rule

Do not solve burnout by working harder inside the same broken system.
