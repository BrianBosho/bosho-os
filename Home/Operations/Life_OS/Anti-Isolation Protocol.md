# Anti-Isolation Protocol

## Warning signs

- I am thinking alone for too long.
- I am reading without discussing.
- I am coding without feedback.
- I am afraid to show incomplete work.
- I have no active collaborator.
- I do not know what my advisor thinks of my direction.
- I am unclear but hiding the lack of clarity.

## Rules

- Every active project needs a feedback source.
- Every two weeks, someone should see the current state of the work.
- Every month, I should talk to someone slightly ahead of me.
- Every semester, I should build or deepen at least one meaningful research relationship.
- I should use CMU and FEUP as networks, not only institutions.

## Action menu

If isolation signs appear, do one of:

- send a project brief to a collaborator
- schedule an altitude check
- attend a reading group
- ask a senior student for 20 minutes
- send an advisor update
- present rough results informally

## Forcing question

> Who should see this before I continue alone?
