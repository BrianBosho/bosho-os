# Major Decisions

This page records the major decisions implied or discussed across the vault.

## Seed Decisions

### Pursue The PhD Seriously

Working conclusion:

- the PhD is close to a no-brainer if it strengthens the right research identity
- it should be treated as leverage, not passive status accumulation

### Optimize For Sustainability Before Entry

Working conclusion:

- do not enter the PhD from burnout, chaos, and depleted recovery bandwidth
- recovery is part of preparation, not a distraction from preparation

### Use A Researcher-Builder Frame

Working conclusion:

- orient toward becoming a credible researcher-builder with technical depth, not just someone who "does a PhD"

### Treat Collaboration As A System

Working conclusion:

- anti-isolation structure must be explicit
- projects need artifacts and people, not only private thought

### Separate Systems From Root Analysis

Working conclusion:

- most immediate progress comes from structure, constraints, and behavior before deeper introspection

## Decisions Still Open

- how the long-term institution-building layer should be staged
