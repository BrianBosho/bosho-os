# 07 Philosophy and Spirituality

## Function

The Buddhist and Vipassana material is part of the operating system for handling ambition, suffering, attention, and detachment.

## Main Uses

- Reinterpret anxiety as conditioned activity.
- Reduce identity-fusion with outcomes.
- Train observation without immediate reaction.
- Ground worth beyond productivity.

## Guardrails

- Do not let spiritual language replace structural fixes.
- Do not turn purity into perfectionism.
- Do not mistake numbness for equanimity.
