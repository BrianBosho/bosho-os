# 08 Health and Energy

## Core Reframe

Manage energy, not only time. Long high-performance days should be treated as different modes of load rather than one undifferentiated block.

## Three-Phase Day

- **Priming**: arrival, warm-up, first deep block.
- **Mid-day engine**: main research and coordination work.
- **Low-gear extension**: wrap-up, writing, debugging, reading, closure.

## Sleep

Fix sleep rhythm before trying to optimize the whole life.

### Main Rule

Protect bedtime. Protect the morning. Reduce late-night decision-making and stimulation. Use rhythm and defaults rather than complicated optimization.

### Relationship to Work

Early clarity is one of the strongest positive signals. The system works best when thinking happens before the day becomes noisy.

## Energy Leaks

- Chronic bracing.
- Unfinished loops.
- Overstimulation.
- Treating all hours as deep-work hours.
- No true off-switch.

## Management Rules

- Front-load the most demanding cognition.
- Distinguish deep work from steady throughput from low-gear maintenance.
- Build micro-releases into the day.
- Protect closure so tension does not accumulate indefinitely.
