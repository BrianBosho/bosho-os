## Work-entry mental simulation

Before a serious block:

1. Imagine starting the task.
2. Notice resistance in body/mind.
3. Name what is present: fear, heaviness, pride, shame, confusion, ambition.
4. Return to the actual output.
5. Start with kindness and seriousness.

## Reflection questions

- Who told me pushing harder would solve my problems?
- Why does technical work matter so much to me?
- Why does a PhD matter?
- Who am I and what do I want?
- What beliefs about worth/work need re-examination?
