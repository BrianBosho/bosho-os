# Vipassana For Anxiety And Pressure

## Core Idea

The problem is not only episodic anxiety. It is a chronic climate of body-mind activation.

## Why Practice Helps

- It conditions safety around previously threatening sensations.
- It reduces the reflex to escape, solve, or suppress immediately.
- It makes the pressure loop more visible in real time.

## Practical Frame

- Hold difficult sensations in awareness.
- Notice story, urgency, and reaction loops.
- Use practice to reduce compulsion, not to become impressive.
