# Detachment And Right Effort

## Detachment Is Not

- Passivity.
- Lack of ambition.
- Emotional numbness.

## Detachment Is

Full engagement in action without tying identity and worth to the outcome.

## Right Effort

- Act clearly.
- Finish the defined thing.
- Do not over-interpret each result.
- Move from performance identity toward careful action.
